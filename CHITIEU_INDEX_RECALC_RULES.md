# CHITIEU Index Recalc Rules

Tai lieu nay ghi lai quy tac bo sung cho man hinh `CHITIEU/CODE/index.php`.

## 1. Cong thuc cot muc tieu

- Khi nguoi dung sua cot `Dieu chinh tang truong`, cot `Chi tieu ke hoach nam ...` phai cap nhat ngay theo cong thuc:
  `Ke hoach nam ... da giao + Dieu chinh tang truong`.
- Quy tac nay ap dung cho ca hien thi tren web, payload gui len server, luu workbook, va xuat DOCX.

## 2. Workbook thieu formula

- Neu workbook goc co o cot muc tieu nhung thieu formula Excel, module van phai tu suy ra cong thuc `da giao + dieu chinh`.
- O muc tieu duoc suy ra theo cong thuc tren phai duoc xem nhu o tinh toan, khong cho sua tay tren man hinh.

## 3. Kiem tra

- Sua mot o `Dieu chinh tang truong` tren `CHITIEU/CODE/index.php` va xac nhan o muc tieu cung dong doi ngay.
- Bam `Luu cap nhat`, tai lai workbook dang xem, va xac nhan gia tri cot muc tieu van dung.
- Xuat DOCX va xac nhan so `ctkh_*` dung theo gia tri `da giao + dieu chinh`.
